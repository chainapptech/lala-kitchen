Chiken pie

British chiken pie, with a full top and bottom crunchy crust,
with the filling made up of small pieces of chicken
and sliced mushrooms in a creamy sauce.
The top crust is typically puff pastry.
Variations on the theme can use nutmeg
or spring onion as part of the creamy filling.
